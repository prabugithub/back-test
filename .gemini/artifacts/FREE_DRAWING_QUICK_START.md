## How to Use (3 Simple Steps)

### Step 1: Click the Pen Icon
Look for the **"Free Draw"** button in the toolbar (it's the pen icon, second from the left).

### Step 2: Draw on the Chart
- Click and hold your mouse where you want to start
- Move your mouse while holding to draw
- Release to finish

### Step 3: Manage Your Drawings
- **Move**: Use the "Select" tool (pointer icon), then drag your drawing
- **Delete**: Select a drawing and click "Delete Selected"
- **Clear All**: Remove all drawings at once
